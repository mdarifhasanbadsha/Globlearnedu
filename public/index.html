About Globlearn

Bridging Ambitions
With Opportunity

Globlearn Education is a premier study-abroad consultancy specialising in China — helping students from across the globe unlock world-class education, government scholarships, and life-changing experiences.

Founded in 2014, we have built deep partnerships with over 120 universities across China. Our team of experienced counsellors guide every student from selecting the right programme to arriving on campus.

How It Works

Your Journey in
6 Simple Steps

01

Free Consultation

Book a free 1-on-1 session with a Globlearn counsellor. We assess your background, goals, and ideal programme.

02

University Selection

We shortlist 3–5 universities matching your profile, budget, and preferred city from our partner network.

03

Application Support

We handle document preparation, translation, personal statement editing, and submit applications on your behalf.

04

Scholarship Application

We identify and apply for all applicable scholarships — CSC, provincial, and university-level funding opportunities.

05

Visa Processing

Our visa experts prepare a complete X1/X2 student visa application with 98% first-attempt success rate.

06

Arrival & Settlement

Airport pickup, campus registration, sim card, bank account — we make sure your first days in China are smooth.
